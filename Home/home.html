Customer Management

Customer Management involves capturing, updating, and deleting customer information to maintain accurate and up-to-date records. It ensures efficient handling of customer details, including identification, contact information, and account status, to provide better service and support.

Transaction Management

Transaction Management involves recording and managing transaction details, including transaction IDs, customer information, and amounts. It ensures accurate tracking of financial activities, helping to maintain clear records and streamline auditing processes.

Loan Management

Loan Management encompasses the process of initiating, updating, and canceling loan requests for customers, including tracking loan status and managing details. It helps streamline the loan application process, ensuring proper handling of customer data, loan terms, and financial details.
